Units
=====

The internal units of :ref:`home` are as follows:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 25 25 25

   * - Quantity
     - Internal Unit
     - User Unit
   * - Length
     - kpc
     - kpc
   * - Velocity
     - kpc/Gyr
     - km/s
   * - Mass
     - Msun
     - Msun
   * - Time
     - Gyr
     - Gyr
   * - Energy
     - Msun (kpc/Gyr)²
     - Msun (km/s)²
   * - Acceleration
     - kpc/Gyr²
     - km/s²
